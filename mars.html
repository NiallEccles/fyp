Mars is the 4th planet from the sun.

Mars has two moons, Phobos and Deimos.
These moons are thought to be captured asteroids.

One day Mars will have a ring
as its moon, Phobos, will be pulled apart.

Mars has seasons like Earth
where there is more sun during the day.

There are signs of liquid water on Mars
beneath the surface and in the ice caps.

Mars has huge dust storms.
These dust storms can cause damage to rovers.

There have been 18 successful missions to Mars.
Not every mission is successful.

Mars is home to the tallest mountain in the solar system.
It is called Olympus Mons.
